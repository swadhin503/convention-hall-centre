Spectra Convention Centre (SCC) is one of the ORIGINAL and LEADING convention centers in Bangladesh. It was established in 2003 with the goal of providing the highest standard of services in the Hospitality Industry. Spectra Convention Centre offers Luxurious Banquet Halls, Business Conference Rooms, Business Centre, all of which that are equipped with a modern hygienic Kitchen, car parking facilities and a fantastic and cozy location in the central business district of Gulshan, Dhaka.

We can provide a wide range of outside catering to suit your personal needs. This could range from a simple business lunch to large event catering. All of our outside catering is made to the same standard that our customers have come to expect from our day delegate conferencing. We work with you to help make sure our event is a successful one!

Spectra Convention Centre, a daughter company of Spectra Group, is a newly raised Catering Services Operator in our country. Being in business for almost twenty years, Spectra Group is involved in different sectors like Construction Industry, Hospital Engineering, Medical Gas Pipe Line System, Microwave Communications and Genetics. As a part of continued diversification, Spectra Group has launched ‘Spectra Catering Limited’ and ‘Spectra Tours and Travels Limited’ with a MISSION to provide better services in Hospitality Industry.
